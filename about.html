About the coach

Twelve years. One craft.

Bio

From the lanes of Alexandria to coaching rooms worldwide.

I started swimming at six and never really stopped. After racing through university and a brief stint on the national circuit, I moved into coaching — first as a side practice, then as my whole life. Today I work with athletes online and in person, from beginners learning to breathe to masters chasing personal bests.

My approach is built on listening first. Every body moves differently in the water, and the job of a coach is to find the version of the stroke that's already trying to come out.
