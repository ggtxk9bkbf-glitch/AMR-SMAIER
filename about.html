The Coach

Amr Samir

Swimming Coach · S&C Specialist · Founder, Fit Zone Gym

Biography

From Mansoura to Kuwait — A Coaching Career Built in the Water.

Amr Samir holds a degree in Physical Education from Mansoura University (2011–2015) and has spent every year since on pool decks and in weight rooms, developing swimmers from youth level to national competition.

In 2024 he took on the role of Assistant Head Coach for Poseidon Kuwait Men — bringing with him over a decade of experience in Egyptian club swimming, an ASCA-equivalent coaching background, and the practical wisdom of founding and running Fit Zone Gym.

His coaching philosophy centres on precise needs assessment, somatic physical development, and the kind of patient, communication-led mentoring that turns potential into consistent performance.
