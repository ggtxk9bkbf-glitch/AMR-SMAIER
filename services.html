The Process

How Your First Month Looks

Wk 0

Discovery Call

Goals, history, current level, schedule. No commitments — just an honest conversation.

Wk 1

Baseline Assessment

Filmed swim session + movement screen on land. We measure everything before building anything.

Wk 2

Plan Delivered

Your written programme — pool sessions, gym work, recovery. Clear, structured, realistic.

Wk 3

First Check-In

Review load, adjust focus. Are you responding? Great. Is something off? We fix it now.

Wk 4

Block Review

Re-film, compare to baseline, measure progress. Plan the next block based on real data.
