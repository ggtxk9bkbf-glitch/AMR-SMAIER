About the Coach

Built on Discipline.

I'm Amr Samir — swimming coach, strength & conditioning specialist, and founder of Fit Zone Gym. Since 2011, I've developed athletes at every level, from youth programmes at Gezira El Ward Club to leading men's squads at Poseidon Kuwait.

My coaching philosophy is simple: understand the athlete, build the programme around their needs, and hold the standard every single session.
